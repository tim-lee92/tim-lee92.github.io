However, after a string of events, I decided that pharmacy was probably not the right path for me.

I took a step back and spent the couple years working various jobs thinking about what I really wanted to do. The idea of going into a field that was completely unknown to me was frightening. I knew that I really liked to solve problems in a logical manner and was somewhat interested in software.

This was back in July of 2016. Since then, I have studied software development and software engineering tirelessly. Through my studies, I have built a solid fundamental in software and have very become proficient in web development and software development.
